Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to foster rigorous interdisciplinary research and enable dialogue between emerging scholars across the humanities and social sciences. We envision a dynamic academic platform that reflects the pulse of contemporary thought while staying rooted in critical traditions.

Empower Emerging Voices

We are committed to spotlighting new thinkers and rising scholars. The journal nurtures academic expression across all disciplines.

Champion Interdisciplinary Research

Our platform encourages dialogue across fields, breaking academic silos. We believe innovation thrives where ideas intersect.

Ensure Scholarly Integrity

Each submission undergoes a rigorous double-blind peer review. We prioritize ethical publishing and academic transparency.

Foster Global Dialogue

We welcome diverse perspectives from around the world. The Continuum seeks to connect ideas across geographies and cultures.

Support Critical Thought

We value original, provocative, and reflective contributions. The journal is a space for thoughtful inquiry and intellectual risk-taking.

Advance Thematic Inquiry

Each issue revolves around timely, thought-provoking themes. We curate content that resonates with current and future scholarly conversations.

6 Steps to Get Published In The Continuum
Visit the "Submit" page for the complete guidelines: Submit An Article

1

Submit your research papers to us via email for initial assessment by our editorial team.

2

Senior editors evaluate the submission for relevance, originality, and adherence to journal guidelines.

3

Manuscripts undergo plagiarism screening and a double-blind peer review where experts assess research depth and novelty.

4

Authors receive feedback, make revisions, and resubmit for final evaluation, if required.

5

Upon approval, the manuscript moves to copyediting and formatting before publication.

6

Your research is published online and in print, indexed for academic accessibility, and promoted through PHI Learning’s networks.
